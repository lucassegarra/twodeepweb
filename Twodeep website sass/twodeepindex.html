Twodeep

Biography

With a highly distinctive approach to sonics and a keen instinct for alternative ways to move the dancefloor, the emergent talent twodeep is a producer rising from the South American underground.

Twodeep’s refined productions defy the short time he has been releasing music, his sound revolves around an alluring, hypnotic groove with a rich tapestry of ambient tones, found sound and instrumentation lingering in between the beats.

Twodeep has already featured tracks on V/A 12”s for Rubisco and Ladeep, while his next solo vinyl only EPs are on the way, featuring remixes like Nick Beringer. Alongside Trentz, he’s supporting local artists by running “Frost Plates Recordings” throwing their local scene worldwide.
